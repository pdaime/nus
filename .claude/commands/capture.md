Capture this into the vault: $ARGUMENTS

1. If $ARGUMENTS is empty, ask what they want to capture (they can paste
   anything, messy is fine).
2. Figure out what it is: class notes → `notes/<class-slug>/`, an idea →
   `notes/ideas/`, a to-do or goal change → update `goals/goals.md`,
   something about them (preference, fact, person) → update
   `memory/profile.md`.
3. Clean it up but keep their meaning and voice. Don't editorialize.
4. Add frontmatter: `title`, `date`, `tags`, plus `class:` if it belongs to
   one. Link related files with [[wikilinks]].
5. If it mentions a deadline not in the semester overview, add it to the
   overview calendar and flag it as user-reported (not from a syllabus).
6. Confirm in one line: what was filed, where. This is how the AI's
   knowledge of them compounds. Every capture makes tomorrow's brief smarter.
