Give the morning brief. Arguments (may be empty): $ARGUMENTS

1. Check today's date.
2. Read `school/semester-overview.md`, `goals/goals.md`, `memory/profile.md`,
   any files in `skills/`, and the most recent 1–2 files in `logs/`.
3. Deliver, compact, in this order:
   - **Today:** anything due today, with class names.
   - **This week:** deadlines in the next 7 days, exams bolded.
   - **Start early:** the one upcoming item (exam, big deliverable, crunch
     week) worth working on *today* even though it isn't due, and say why.
   - **Leveling:** one line on their active skill track (`skills/`): what
     today's rep is, per the current level.
   - **Focus:** ONE sentence, the single most leveraged thing today.
4. If yesterday's quests went unfinished, carry the important one forward in
   one line. No guilt-tripping.
5. Whole brief under ~15 lines, readable in 30 seconds. Match the tone they
   chose in `memory/profile.md`.
